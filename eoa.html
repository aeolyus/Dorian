Explanation of Assignment

Submitters: Richard Huang, Krystal Tien, Pauline, Andrew Tran, Riya Butani, Bryant Tran

Assignment Name: Project Dorian Scale

Nature of Assignment: Survey/Visual

Number of Hours Applied to Completion of Assignment: 24 hours (in total)

Description of Assignment: Based on people’s responses on our self-created morality survey, we took Kohlberg’s Scale and represented their position of the scale by using paint. Everyone starts with black paint and depending on their position on the scale, a certain amount of white paint drops was added to the black.

Statement of Purpose of Assignment: We wanted to apply what we learned in Campfire and show that people aren’t simply black and white. In fact, everyone is a different shade of grey, like how Dorian Gray is a different shade than someone like Lord Henry. By using Kohlberg’s Scale, we tested our knowledge of each stage by applying it to subjective, controversial questions.
